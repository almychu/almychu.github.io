About

Hi! I'm Albert, a graduating senior at PEA.

Albert is a senior in Dow House at Phillips Exeter Academy. He’s originally from California, although he’s spent time living abroad in Shanghai and Suzhou, China as well.

Albert is passionate about space and robotics! He developed software for the MIT STAR Lab’s DemiSat last summer, is a Co-President of Exeter’s three robotics teams for SeaPerch, MATE, and FTC, and loves to whittle and tinker around with various electronics.

Albert co-founded the Public Forum Debate team at Exeter and is a three-time Nationals qualifier. He also speaks Chinese, English and Latin, and he’s currently learning Ancient Greek as well. He also knows a few more current languages in the form of programming languages: Python, Java, and a little C++. Please take a look around, and reach out if you have any comments or questions!
